About Me

My name is Nicholas Brillant, but I go by Nick. I repeated my junior year of high school to attend and play lacrosse at The Taft School in Connecticut. I am incredibly interested in pursuing the majors of business and computer science in college. I have always been a business-minded person. I pride myself on my amiability and ability to engage in conversation with those I have just met, and for years I have kept an ever-growing list of prospective business ideas that I would like to pursue. Throughout my high school career, I have attempted to enhance my proclivity for business by taking courses that will prepare me for a career in such a field. In my junior year at JH Rose high school, I took a business law course at my local community college. I performed very well in the class and subsequently searched for more courses that would sharpen me for the business world. At Taft I have taken both personal finance and marketing classes and I am currently enrolled in AP economics (micro and macro). I have found these courses riveting and they have provided me with countless tools to bring into my future profession.

Due to the unbridled realm of creative possibility computer science provides, integrating an obsession with business will inevitably yield incredible results. Combining the two fields that I have been so immersed in would be an incredible opportunity that I hope to explore within the UM Foote Fellows Honors program.
